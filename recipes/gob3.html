Gob3

Beans and Gari

Gob3 is basically a Ghanaian delicacy made up of beans and gari

It also has palm oil in it and is eaten with fried riped plantain

Ingredients

  • beans
  • gari
  • palm oil
  • fried plantain

Steps

  • Put beans into the cooking bowl
  • Pour water into the bowl content
  • Pour palm oil and gari on the cooked beans
  • Add the fried plantain and enjoy
