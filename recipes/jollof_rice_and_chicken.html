Jollof rice and chicken

Jollof rice and chicken is a Ghanaian dish basically made up of rice stew mixture and chicken

The chicken is fried and added to the cooked rice stew mixture

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • oil
  • tomatoes

Steps

  1. Prepare the stew in a bowl
  2. Add water and top up to some extent
  3. Add the rice and cook for some 15 to 20 minutes
  4. Fry the chicken and add to the cooked jollof and enjoy
