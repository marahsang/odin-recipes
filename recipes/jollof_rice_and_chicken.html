Jollof rice and chicken

Jollof rice and chicken is a Ghanaian dish basically made up of rice stew mixture and chicken

The chicken is fried and added to the cooked rice stew mixture

Ingredients

  • rice
  • chicken
  • oil
  • tomatoes

Steps

  • Prepare the stew in a bowl
  • Add water and top up to some extent
  • Add the rice and cook for some 15 to 20 minutes
  • Fry the chicken and add to the cooked jollof and enjoy
