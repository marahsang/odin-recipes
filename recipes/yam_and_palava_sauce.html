Yam and palava sauce

Yam and palava sauce is basically a Ghanaian dish mainly constituting yam and palava sauce

The palava sauce is prepared with cocoyam leaves(kontomire) and ground melon seeds(egushi)

Ingredients

  • yam
  • ground melon seeds
  • cocoyam leaves
  • palm oil

Steps

  • Peel off the yam
  • Cut into slices and add water in a bowl
  • Boil for about 20 minutes and cooked
  • Add the ground cocoyam leaves and the melon seeds in a palm oil bowl content
