Yam and Palaver sauce

Yam and Palaver sauce is basically a Ghanaian dish mainly constituting yam and palava sauce

The Palaver sauce is prepared with cocoyam leaves(kontomire) and ground melon seeds(egushi)

Ingredients

  • yam
  • ground melon seeds
  • cocoyam leaves
  • palm oil

Steps

  1. Peel off the yam
  2. Cut into slices and add water in a bowl
  3. Boil for about 20 minutes and cooked
  4. Add the ground cocoyam leaves and the melon seeds in a palm oil bowl content
